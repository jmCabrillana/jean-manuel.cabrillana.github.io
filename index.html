About Me

Hello, I'm Jean-Manuel. I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Interdisciplinary Graduate Programme AI for X (IGP-AI-X) at Nanyang Technological University, under the supervision of Asst. Prof. Yoonchang Sung. My research focuses on planning and control for robotics in high-dimensional interactive environments—particularly meta-learning, memory-augmented architectures for long-horizon decision-making, and reinforcement learning for robust generalization and adaptation.

I hold a dual M.Sc. in Machine Learning, AI & Data (Sorbonne University) and in Executive Engineering (Mines Saint-Étienne). Before starting my PhD, I spent several years in industry as a data scientist and ML engineer in Paris and Hong Kong.

I'm always happy to chat about research or collaboration — feel free to reach out!
